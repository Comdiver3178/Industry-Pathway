About

Real-world context, not theory

MOS Maritime Group Limited provides structured advisory for aspiring and developing offshore commercial divers. The focus is pathway clarity, cost control, sequencing, and realistic offshore positioning.

This is designed for people who want to avoid expensive mistakes, stop relying on guesswork, and make better decisions before committing serious money to training and tickets.

Advisory only — no recruitment representation, no employment placement, and no work guarantees.
