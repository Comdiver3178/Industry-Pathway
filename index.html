About

MOS Maritime Group Limited is a New Zealand-based offshore advisory practice founded by Matthew John Turner — a commercial saturation diver and IMCA Air Diving Supervisor.

This is not motivational coaching. This is structured maritime career strategy.

Commercial diving is capital-intensive, physically demanding and contract-driven. Poor sequencing of training, medical certification and offshore positioning can cost significant money and time.

MOS Maritime Group exists to provide clarity before major financial and career decisions are made — focusing on discipline, structure and long-term progression.
